VSound Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

VSound app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. This page is used to inform the users about the policies with regard to the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information.

Information Collection and Use

VSound app does not collect any personal information, does not make recording of any of the sound being analysed, and does not request access to Internet, and therefore has no way of sharing your information with anyone, including the developer.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. This policy is effective as of 2020-04-15.
